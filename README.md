# Insurance-Risk-Claims-Analysis
The Insurance Risk & Claims Analysis Dashboard is a Power BI solution designed to help insurance companies analyze policyholder risk profiles, monitor claim trends, and improve decision-making through data-driven insights. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of claims, premiums, customer demographics, and risk factors to support underwriting and claims management.

Project Objectives
Analyze insurance claim patterns and trends.
Identify high-risk customer segments.
Monitor claim frequency and claim severity.
Evaluate policy performance and profitability.
Support underwriting and risk assessment decisions.
Detect potential fraud indicators and unusual claim behavior.
